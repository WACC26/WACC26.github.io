1. Protocol: The set of rules that govern how data is sent.
  2. Packet: The things data are broken up into so that they may be sent over the internet.
  3. Header: The added data that tells the internet where the packet needs to go.
  4. Payload: The actual data being sent.
  5. Router: A device that connects several networks.
  6. Routing table: A routers map of the internet.
