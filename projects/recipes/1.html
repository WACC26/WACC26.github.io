Easy Fudge Recpie

You need three ingredients for this recpie, Chocolate chips, Sweetend Condensed Milk, and Vanilla Extract

  1. Combine the choclate chips and milk in a small sauce pan. Place the pan on low heat.
  2. Stir until chocolate melts. After it melts stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Contine to stir until it is all melted.
  4. Pour into a 8 by 8 pan. Let the fudge set at room temp for four hours.
  5. Cut the fudge, then serve.
