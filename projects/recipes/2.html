Sweet Noodle Kugel

Ingredients

  1. 8 ounce of widge egg noodles
  2. 2 cups cottage cheese
  3. 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  4. 1/2 cup white suger
  5. 1/2 cup raisins
  6. 3 large eggs, beaten
  7. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  9. 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Boil water in a large pot, boil egg noodles
  3. Mix cheese, sour cream, sugar, raisins, eggs, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a large bowl. Then, mix in cooked noodles.
  4. Bake for about 40 minutes.
